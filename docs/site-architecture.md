# Site Architecture and Wireframe Notes

## Navigation
- Home (`index.html`)
- About (`about.html`)
- Services (`services.html`)
- FAQs (`faqs.html`)
- Contact (`contact.html`)

Secondary footer navigation:
- Privacy Policy (`privacy.html`)
- Terms (`terms.html`)
- Medical Disclaimer (`medical-disclaimer.html`)

## Shared Layout Pattern
1. Sticky header with navigation and primary CTA ("Book Consultation")
2. Hero or page heading section
3. Main content sections (cards, lists, and trust blocks)
4. CTA strip before footer
5. Footer with contact, hours, and legal links

## Wireframe by Page

### Home
- Hero: clinic positioning, concise credibility statement, dual CTA
- Service highlights: 3-5 key service cards
- Why choose us: privacy, evidence-based care, expert clinicians
- Team preview and testimonial/trust statements
- Final CTA strip

### About
- Clinic mission and values
- Practitioner bios with qualifications
- Care approach and process overview

### Services
- Intro on treatment philosophy
- Detailed cards for each service
- "What to expect" consultation process
- Safety/disclaimer note

### FAQs
- Accordion-style question groups:
  - Privacy and confidentiality
  - Appointments and wait times
  - Billing and payment
  - Treatment expectations

### Contact
- Contact channels (call, email, address)
- Embedded map link
- Clinic hours
- Inquiry / appointment request form with consent checkbox

## Content and UX Rules
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable.
- Keep all critical CTAs above the fold on mobile.
- Avoid absolute medical outcome claims.
- Use clear accessibility labels for form controls.
